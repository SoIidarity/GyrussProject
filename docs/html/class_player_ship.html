Detailed Description

playerShip Class, inherited from Entity

A playerShip is the player avatar, a spaceship which rotates around the perimeter of a circle. A playerShip can shoot and move left or right. The ship spawns at the bottom of the movement circle. The ship is moved by a change in angle, and is translated by mod/arg form, with a fixed radius determined as a percentage of the vertical screen resolution.
