Implementation of Enemy ship types.

Parameters
resolutionThe screen resolution (to determine the radius the playerShip circles around
distanceFromCentreThe fixed radius of the circle that the ship flies around
angleThe angle in degrees around the circle of movement
scaleThe scale of the sprite
typeAn enum that identifies the Enemy type. Defined in common.hpp, passed to the Animatable base class
textureHolderThe textureHolder object, passed by const ref. provides a unique_ptr to an sf::Texture for the sprite data member
idThe unique ID identifying the texture for the Enemy variant
movementStateEnum identifying the pattern of movement for the Enemy
movementDirectionEnum for the movement direction (clockwise or counterclockwise)

◆ setOrientation()

void Enemy::setOrientation ( )

Sets the orientation of the Enemy screen object.

The previous and future positions are converted to vectors at the origin, and the final position is then subtracted from the initial position, to determine the new pointing vector. Then the sprite of the enemy is rotated to match this direction
