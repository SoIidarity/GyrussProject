9 ## *Latest Release: v3.0* <br>
10 * A space sh'mup clone, based on Taito Corporation's 1984 Gyruss
11 * Written by Tyson Cross and Chris Maree for ELEN3018 Software Engineering 2
12 * Control the ship with the *left* and *right* arrow keys
13 * Shoot bullets with the *space bar*
14 * *Ctrl-Q* quits (or close the window)
15 * Points are scored by killing enemies, and by surviving for as long as possible
16 * Written in C++14, using [Simple and Fast Multimedia Library \[SFML 2.4.2\]](https://www.sfml-dev.org/)
17 
18 Run the game by opening executables/game.exe <br>
19 
20 Requires Windows 7/8/10 and a display capable of 1920x1080 fullscreen
